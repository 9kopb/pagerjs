Sending the Wildcard to Source

The page ID that matched the wildcard can be sent to the source: or sourceOnShow:. Just add {1} inside the source: or sourceOnShow: and {1} will be replaced by the page ID.

Go to Fry 
  1. <div data-bind="page: {id: 'start'}">
  2.     <a href="#!/send_wildcard_to_source/character/fry">Go to Fry</a>
  3. </div>
  4.  
  5. <div data-bind="page: {id: 'character'}">
  6.     <h3>Character</h3>
  7.  
  8.     <div data-bind="page: {id: '?', sourceOnShow: 'character/{1}.html'}">
  9.     </div>
  10. </div>
  11.
